02 · Legacy

Seventy years of champions.

Olympians. Commonwealth Games. National champions. Deaflympians. Three generations of Melbourne shooters trained on these lines.

1955

Founded

Melbourne International Shooting Club established as a metropolitan ISSF pistol & rifle club.

1976

Todd Road home

MISC moves to its permanent home at 120-128 Todd Road, Port Melbourne — purpose-built ranges.

1996

Air range development

10m air range built with electronic targets — a national first for a metropolitan club.

1997

ISSF rifle introduced

MISC becomes a leading Victorian club for full Olympic-discipline rifle competition.

2005

Deaflympics

MISC members compete and medal at the Deaflympics — a club-defining moment.

2012

Ragnar Skanåker Grand Prix

Hosting the international Grand Prix — Olympic-pathway shooters compete on home lines.

2026

Junior to the Olympics

A MISC junior member joins the Olympic team — the legacy continues.

Coached fundamentals.
